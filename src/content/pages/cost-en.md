## Typical project ranges

- **Mobile MVP (iOS + Android):** $40K–$120K — depends on backend complexity, integrations, and design polish.
- **Web platform:** $30K–$200K — varies enormously by data model, user roles, and integrations.
- **AI agent (single workflow):** $15K–$60K — discovery + 14-day prototype + production launch with SOSA controls.
- **Dedicated staffing:** $7K–$15K per engineer per month, based on seniority and stack.

## What drives the number up

- Custom backend or compliance requirements (HIPAA, SOC 2, accessibility audits).
- Real-time or AI-heavy features that need careful architecture.
- Multiple integrations with legacy systems.
- Tight timelines — accelerated delivery costs more, always.

## What we can flex on

- **Phased delivery** — ship the highest-value 30% in V1, validate, then expand.
- **Mixed-seniority team** — pair senior architects with mid-level builders.
- **Existing codebase** — if you have something to start from, we don't rebuild what works.

## How we quote

1. **Free discovery call** — 30-60 min to understand scope.
2. **Written estimate within 5 business days** — fixed-fee for defined scope, T&M for exploratory.
3. **No surprise invoices** — anything outside the agreed scope is flagged + quoted before we do it.
