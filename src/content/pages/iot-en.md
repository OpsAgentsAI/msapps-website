## What we build

- **Apps for medical hardware** — Device integration, patient monitoring, alerting, regulated data handling.
- **Fleet and vehicle platforms** — Driver apps, fleet manager dashboards, OBD / CAN bus integration.
- **Smart home and business devices** — Central control of sensors, lighting, security.
- **Industrial sensors** — Field data ingestion, visualization, alerting.

## Why IoT is its own discipline

IoT projects don't tolerate integration sloppiness — when the app doesn't talk to the hardware, the product doesn't work. We build for this: real-hardware testing, simulated network failures, and graceful handling of real-world error states. Our team is used to working hand-in-hand with hardware engineers at every phase.
